以下是建议

Based on your answers in the Options tab, this is the best distribution of your relics across the non-maxable artifacts. It is presumed that you will max out any artifact that can be, which is why they are not included in this view. It doesn't need to be 100% precise, but this is the general shape that is most effective. At higher artifact levels, I find 1% to be the best way to get as close as possible to these numbers.

NOTE: Recently switch builds or switch to this method of optimizing? Don't worry if they don't exactly match for several prestiges. Over time it will smooth out and get on track. In the meantime just get them as close as you can! I recommend that after you get BoS to the right level, you alternate each prestige from starting at the top or bottom of the list. It's the fastest way to get back on track while maintaining good balance.
